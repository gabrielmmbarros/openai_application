Product Name: Natural Shampoo  
Review Summary: The natural shampoo enhances hair softness and shine, delivering visible aesthetic benefits. However, its cleaning efficiency is less than conventional shampoos, especially for oily hair types, making it unsuitable for everyone.  
Overall Sentiment: Neutral  
Strengths:  
- Leaves hair soft and shiny.  
- Natural formulation.  
- Gentle on hair and scalp.  
Weaknesses:  
- Limited cleaning effectiveness.  
- Not ideal for oily hair types.  
- May not meet expectations compared to conventional shampoos.
